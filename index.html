The Secret to Tech Career Success

UNLOCKED!

I surveyed over 200 "Tech-Adjacent" people -project managers, business liaisons, and executives- on the difference between good technologists and the BEST they ever worked with. The results were surprising...

The Difference Between Good and Great

TECH SKILLS ARE NOT THE DIFFERENCE-MAker

...not one respondent mentioned technical knowledge as the difference between good technologists and the BEST they've ever worked with.

We all spend more time and effort honing our technical skills- and too little energy developing the skills that will actually make us great. 

We've GOT TO CHANGE OUR HABITS

It's Time to Reconsider Soft Skills

Don't just take it from me - here's what experts say:

"In a high-IQ job pool, soft skills like discipline, drive and empathy mark those who emerge as outstanding." -Daniel Goleman 

"Skills are important but when I look for very senior employees, I always prioritize EQ (Emotional Intelligence) over hard skills." -Gary Vaynerchuk

 "Let’s call them real skills, not soft...Real because they work, because they’re at the heart of what we need to do today...Real skills can’t replace vocational skills, of course not. What they can do is amplify the things you’ve already been measuring." -Seth Godin

Soft Skills are just as IMPORTANT as Tech Skills
